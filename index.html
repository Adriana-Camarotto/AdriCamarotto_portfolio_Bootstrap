About me

Hello! As a passionate about technology, I started my first steps studying logic programming during the civil engineering degree, which for many reasons I did not take forward.

Years later, I went to university to do a business degree, and after it and I undertook in a start-up for five years. During this process I had a daughter, also passionate about technology. To help her, I ended up mentoring several teams for three years of the Technovation Challenge - a worldwide programming challenge for girls - even without knowing how to program, but with a good business knowledge. And I was so proud of all my teams in special because those girls were coding and one of the team in 2018 was the South American Champion of the Techovantion Challenge!

Well, well time flies and after immigrating to the UK, working in various areas, venturing into digital marketing, mentoring women's business, I ended up missing this wonderful world of technology and here I am starting my journey in programming with the aim of becoming a front end developer. And I can tell you that I'm loving putting my hand in the dough!
